About

In this pandemic situation, primary precautionary way is to maintain Social Distancing. Here, we developed a Social Distancing System, that will generate an alert sound whenever two or more people come into close contact.
The complete project is prepared in Python using OpenCV. We developed the project in Jupyter Notebook evironment. Here we used Haar Cascade classifier for human detection. Then calculated the Euclidean Distance between the humans detected and applied some mathematical constants to measure the distance in reality.

Requirements:
